**********
Web Client
**********

Using OMERO.web to view and work with image data via a web browser.

You need to use the URL provided by your institute’s OMERO administrator to access the OMERO.web client.

Logging in, you do not have to enter a server name, but otherwise it is the same as for OMERO.insight, using the same username and password.

1. The default screen shows your data tree in the left-hand panel.
When data is selected in the tree, the thumbnails are displayed in the centre panel, and the metadata and other information in the right-hand panel. 

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientHome.png
    :width: 75%
    :align: center

2. Select an image in the data tree and click on the **Launch Full Viewer** button to view it in the **Image Viewer**;
or. double click on the image or thumbnail.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientView.png
    :width: 87.5%
    :align: center

3. In the Image Viewer images can be viewed in **Normal**, **Maximum Intensity** or **Split-Channel**. 

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientViewerOptions.png
    :width: 100%
    :align: center

4. Scroll or step through Z sections using the slider and controls to the left of the image and T sequences using the slider and controls below the image. 

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientViewerSliders.png
    :width: 87.5%
    :align: center

5. Zoom in and out of the image using the up and down arrows in the text box.
Click the 1:1 button to view at true size.
Click the diagonal arrow button to zoom to fit the window.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientViewerZoom.png
    :width: 75%
    :align: center

6. Click on **Edit** to open the **Rendering Details** window.
Use the sliders or text boxes to adjust settings for the channels.
Toggle channels and colour on and off using the colour wells and checkbox.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientViewerRendering.png
    :width: 100%
    :align: center

7. Click on **Image Information** to see the basic information and dimensions of the image.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientViewerInfo.png
    :width: 100%
    :align: center

8. Click on **Image Link** to generate a URL which can be copied and pasted.
This link provides direct access via a web browser to your image, allowing people who are not in your group or even OMERO users to view your image.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientViewerSliders.png
    :width: 70%
    :align: center

9. Click on **Show ROIs** to view ROIs on the image and see details in the pop-up window (only visible if image has ROIs).
Existing ROIs can be viewed but cannot be edited, and new ROIs cannot added.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientViewerROI.png
    :width: 100%
    :align: center

10. In the **General** tab of the right hand panel, click on the pencil icon to edit the image name or image description.
Summary details of the image can be seen below the description.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientGeneral1.png
    :width: 75%
    :align: center

11. Click on the **Link** button to generate a URL to share the image (as described previously).

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientGeneral2.png
    :width: 75%
    :align: center

12. Click on the + button to add a Tag or Attachment.
You can attach existing tags or files, create and attach new tags or upload and attach files from discs.
Click on **Accept** to complete.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientGeneral3.png
    :width: 100%
    :align: center

13. Enter a new comment in the text box and click on the **Add Comment** button to save.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientGeneral4.png
    :width: 70%
    :align: center

14. If you belong to more than one group, you can interact with the data of other users in your group. 
Click on the drop-down arrow to the right of your name in the bar above the data tree, and select the desired group and user.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientGroups.png
    :width: 100%
    :align: center

15. You can also share data with other users in different groups by adding it to your **Public** list.
Select the image/s you wish to share.
Click on the **Basket** icon in the toolbar.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientShare1.png
    :width: 75%
    :align: center

16. A red number, representing the number of shared images, will appear in the Basket icon to the right side of the top toolbar.
Click on the numbered **Basket** button to view a list of shared items in the basket.
Click on the **Manage Basket** button in the drop-down list to view the images.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientShare2.png
    :width: 100%
    :align: center

17. Select an image and click on the **Globe** button in the **Basket** window to share it.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientShare3.png
    :width: 100%
    :align: center

18. In the **Share** window add a message and select the members you wish to share the data with.
Click **Accept** when you have finished.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientShare4.png
    :width: 100%
    :align: center

19. Click on the **Public** tab of the left hand panel to view the data shared with you and other users.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientShare5.png
    :width: 100%
    :align: center

20. From the OMERO.web client, you can download and run a **Web Start** version of OMERO.insight.
Using the **Web Start** OMERO.insight client enables you to import data, edit ROIs and use other functionality not yet supported by the OMERO.web client.
**Web Start** also enables OMERO.insight client to be used on machines where restrictions prevent you from installing new software.

Click on the **Web Start** button and follow the instructions, accepting the software as “Trusted”.

.. image:: /images/tutor/webclientWebStart.png
    :width: 100%
    :align: center
