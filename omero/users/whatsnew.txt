What's new for OMERO 5
======================

From a user's perspective, OMERO 5 mostly alters the way import looks in 
OMERO.insight:

- :doc:`client-tutorials/insight/importing-data` has been updated to 
  illustrate the new workflow.

- Screenshots throughout the user guides have been updated.

:doc:`demo-server` is not available for OMERO 5 yet.
