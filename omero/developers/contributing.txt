Contributing to OMERO
=====================

This section includes various resources for anyone wanting to contribute
code to OME. These include 'standard procedure' for various tasks such as
testing, documentation and release, investigations into new work or just
internal documents that may be of interest to a wider audience. If you have
any questions, please see our :doc:`/users/community-resources` for ways
to get in touch.
