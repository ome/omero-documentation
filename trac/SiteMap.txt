`SiteMap </ome/wiki/SiteMap>`_
==============================

This page is an overview of the main areas of development documentation
on the trac. Wiki pages which are not reachable from this page should
not be considered significant, and may be in-progress or for historical
reasons only (i.e. deprecated). You can find such pages via the
`TitleIndex </ome/wiki/TitleIndex>`_.

Before a new page is added, this page should be consulted to find a
similar page with which the content may could be merged. If no such page
is found, find the most appropriate spot in the
`SiteMap </ome/wiki/SiteMap>`_ and list the newly created page.

**Note: information on how to keep this page up-to-date and how to name,
place wiki pages is being worked on via `#7035 </ome/ticket/7035>`_.**
